In the Ostrog Bible script, the Kendema was also used both as a combining and a stand-alone character to indicate marginal readings or comments. The Kendema is used over the Izhitsa as well as in some other instances, for example, to indicate the dual of some words, or as a scripture verse divider, as in the Ostrog Bible. Note U+0477 above.
